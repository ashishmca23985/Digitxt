    Stop Unwanted Messages

    The quickest way to block someone from sending messages to you via BulkSMS.com is to reply to the sender's SMS with the word STOP. Alternatively, this facility enables you to find out who messaged you via our gateway and block the offending user from messaging you again.

    Stop Unwanted Messages

    We will show you all recent messages sent to your mobile via our gateway. You can then block individual users of ours from messaging you, as well as obtain contact details of the original sender.

    To protect the privacy of our users we first need to send a code to your mobile to verify your mobile number. After you confirm this code, you will be shown all messages sent to you through our systems for the past 14 days, and the details of the users who sent them.
